# Blog Voice Profile

Everything on, strict mode. Blog posts are where AI voice is most noticeable and most damaging to credibility. This profile pushes hard on personal voice, concrete specifics, and authentic tone.

## Tier 2 Guidance: Language Patterns

Zero tolerance for AI language in blog posts:
- Flag every buzzword: readers spot "delve" and "realm" instantly
- Flag every hedge: blog readers want opinions, not "it could be argued"
- Flag every filler: tighten every sentence
- Flag meta-commentary hard: "in this post" is the #1 tell for AI-generated blogs

## Tier 3 Guidance: Structural Patterns

Blog-specific structural checks:
- Flag any section that is entirely bullet points: blog posts should flow as prose
- Flag rule-of-three: readers notice the pattern after seeing it twice
- Flag "from X to Y": this construction screams AI, especially in intros
- Flag monotonous sentence length: blogs need rhythm, short punchy statements mixed with longer explanations
- Flag over-balanced takes: blog posts should have a point of view

## Tier 4 Guidance: Voice Patterns

This is the most important tier for blogs:
- Flag every passive construction: blogs should feel direct and personal
- Flag every generic analogy: "think of it like..." is lazy; use a real example
- Demand first-person perspective where appropriate: "I found", "we built", "our team"
- Flag vague claims: "significantly faster" must become "50ms down from 200ms"
- Flag any opening that doesn't hook: the first sentence should make someone want the second
- Flag false enthusiasm: "exciting", "amazing", "incredible" without substance
